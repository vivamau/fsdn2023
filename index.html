Solutions' health: status analysis

Once a solution is reaching our team, it got a status that is going to be used to updated the progress of the analysis or of the support of the application during the FSDN journey. So the application can be in a under review status (43 (+16 since 2021)), or be ready for the Architectural Board review (none), or ready to go on production (10, +7) or in production (6 (+3)). An application then can be also discountinued (6 (+4)) or cancelled (4) because a corporate solution is now available. In addition 29 entries have been created but no action taken yet.

Solutions' quality: score analysis

In order to be able to put applications in a global context, we have introduced a score. Based on the different aspects, from documentation, to security, to hosting, to adeherence to WFP standards, the score trying to provide a good sense of what the quality of an application is, how far is the application to be acceptable and to do not be a threath for the organization. In this sense the score has to reach at least 60 points over 100 available.

  • Below 39
  • 40 - 59
  • Above 60

In this sense the score has to reach at least 60 points over 100 available. At the moment the number of applications that are over 60 is 22 (+15 since 2021, greater improvement). The applications between 40 and 59 are 11, while the applications below 39 are 22.

The average score for the 98 applications is 34 (+2 since 2021). In RBB is 38 (+16) (for 9 applications), in RBC is 29 (+5) (for 62 applications), in RBD is 38 (for 7 applications) and in RBJ is 68 (for 4 applications), in RBN is 37 (for 4 solutions), in RBN is 33 (for 4 solutions).
